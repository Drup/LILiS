Module Mini_calc

module Mini_calc: sig .. end
Small library to evaluate simple arithmetic expressions.

This library evaluates simple arithmetic expression over floats. Regular operators (+,-,*,/,^) and some regular functions (sin, cos, tan, asin, acos, atan, log, log10, exp, sqrt) are implemented. Arithmetic expressions can contains variables.

Here is an example of expression : 3*x+sin(2) .
